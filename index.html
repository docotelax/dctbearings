About Us

DCT Bearings is a trusted supplier of industrial components to South African manufacturers. We specialize in providing high-quality bearings, gearboxes, motors, belts, and linear motion systems for industries such as food processing, paper manufacturing, ceramics, and more.

Our Products

  • Ball, Roller & Needle Bearings
  • Linear Bearings & Shafts
  • Gearboxes & Electric Motors
  • V-Belts, Timing Belts & Pulleys
  • Couplings, Sprockets & Chains
  • Seals, Lubricants & Tools

Industries We Serve

We support a wide range of industries, including:

  • Food & Beverage (e.g., biscuit manufacturing)
  • Paper & Tissue Production
  • Tile & Ceramic Manufacturing
  • Mining & Heavy Industry
  • Automotive & Engineering
